About Me

My name is Margaret Hu. I am a Taiwanese-American digital artist based in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Personal Life

I graduated a few years ago with a Bachelor's degree in computer science. I originally wanted to be a software developer in the video game industry, where I could contribute to creating stunning designs and innovative gameplay mechanics. About a month after graduation, the economy entered a period of recession. Since then, I have continued searching for tech work while engaging in digital art in my spare time.

Art Style

My artwork often draws inspiration from East Asian culture, stunning landscapes, and immersive storytelling. I love incorporating vibrant colors, intricate details, and playful elements to create a surreal realm where reality and imagination intertwine. I hope my art evokes a sense of wonder and brings you joy.
